Taiwan is a small country and most of the food can be found all over the country. However, each city still has its own specialty. For example, bubble milk tea can be found everywhere, the origin store of Chun Shui Tang which was the first tea shop invented bubble milk tea is only located in Taichung. Each food has its own history and story to share. Some foods can be found in street vendors or night markets, which makes it more interesting to look for the food you want to try.
