First time visiting Taiwan and doesn’t know where to go?
Don’t worry, let’s figure out what you want to eat or drink first, and we will think about where we want to go later. However, the only thing you MUST try is the BUBBLE MILK TEA.


FUN FACT :
Did you know?
Taiwan invented the first boba in the world!
In 1949, Chang Fan Shu opened the first tea shop, and the tea was made with a cocktail shaker. Now, it has become a global addiction that everyone cannot live without.


Here are some different types of boba:

  • Classic Bubble Milk Tea
  • Brown Sugar Milk Tea
  • Thai Tea with Boba
  • Milk Tea Pudding
  • Taro Bubble Tea
  • Fruit Tea

Types of Tea:

  • Black Tea
  • Oolong Tea
  • Green Tea
  • White Tea

Types of Boba:

  • Black boba
  • Crystal boba
  • Mini boba
  • Popping boba
  • Tapioca boba

Bubble milk tea in the U.S. usually is too sweet, even less sugar is still too sweet for most customers. It is very unhealthy. However, the bubble tea shops in Taiwan will adjust the sweetness that customers want.
